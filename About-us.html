Our Story

Baked with Heart,
Served with Pride.

For over two decades, Palace Bakery has been the morning ritual of thousands of families across Kumasi — and beyond.

Where It All Began

From a Small Kitchen
to a Beloved Bakery.

Palace Bakery was founded in 2020 by Mariama Alhassan, a self-taught baker who started selling bread from her kitchen window in Atonsu, Kumasi. What began as a neighbourhood favour quickly became a daily tradition.

Word spread — loaf by loaf, family by family — until demand outgrew her kitchen. By 2021, Palace had its first storefront. Today, we operate multiple locations and bake over 500 pies a day, all still by hand.

The recipe hasn't changed. The love hasn't either.
